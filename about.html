About Me

My name is Allen Zhang and come from China. Currently, I work full-time as a glass window tint installer and also take on freelance projects in caricature drawing, car wrapping, and car window tinting. Of all my jobs, I enjoy car wrapping the most because I enjoy the process to change the car color and make it look cool and protected. I have always been like adding protections to staff. I add protected film to my phone, my switch, even covers for my game controllers. I have a background as a 3D animator to make animated movie or cartons. I have doing caricature drawing for past 7 years. I used to work as fulltime caricature artist at the zoo, six flag, and other attractions. I start working as freelancer after my kid was worn and he turn six years old this year. He is a lovely, happy kid. I love him so much. Outside of work, I enjoy swimming, snowboarding, and collecting anime figures. Recently, I joined a soccer team and have been play soccer every Saturday when I am free. I really enjoy playing with my friend. When I get a break, I love playing FIFA on PlayStation at home. I love snowboarding because I enjoy the speed and freedom. My biggest goal is to one day open my own car wrapping shop. Balancing work, freelancing, and family life is a challenge, especially as a father to a lively six-year-old boy, but I stay motivated by my passion for what I do.
